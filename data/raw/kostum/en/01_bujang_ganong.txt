Bujangganong is one of the principal Reog dancers, portraying the young prime minister (patih) of King Klono Sewandono. He is known for being agile, clever, humorous, and powerful. The dancer’s costume is usually open and dominated by red or black, often embellished with fringes or knitted ornaments in bright red or yellow.
Properties used by Bujangganong include:
 A red mask, red vest, trousers, embong gombyog (tassels), stagen, epek timang, red and yellow sampur (shawls), red vest with black stripes, and binggel (bracelets).
