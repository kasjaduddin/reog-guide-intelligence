Jathil depicts soldiers training for war and playing while riding horses. This dance is performed while carrying a kepang horse, which is a horse made of woven bamboo painted to resemble a real horse. The jathil character is agile and assertive, but sometimes weak and graceful. The costumes and props used by jathil include:
1. Panji border pants
2. White shirt
3. Jarit parang srimpi
4. Boro-boro and samir
5. Stagen
6. Epek timang belt
7. Red sampur
8. Yellow sampur
9. Kace
10. Srempang
11. Cakep
12. Binggel
13. Udeng
14. Eblek (horse)
