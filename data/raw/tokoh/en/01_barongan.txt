Barongan (Dadak Merak) is the most dominant dance equipment in Reog Ponorogo art. Its parts include the Tiger Head (Caplokan), made of a wooden frame, bamboo, and rattan covered with Gembong tiger skin. Dadak merak, the frame is made of bamboo and rattan as a place to arrange peacock feathers to depict a peacock spreading its feathers and biting a string of beads (tasbih). Krakap is made of black velvet fabric embroidered with monte, which is an accessory and a place to write the identity of the Reog group. This peacock headdress is approximately 2.25 meters long, 2.30 meters wide, and weighs almost 50 kilograms.
