“Warok,” which comes from the word wewarah, is a person who has pure determination, provides guidance and protection selflessly. Warok is wong kang sugih wewarah (a person who is rich in wewarah), meaning someone who is able to give guidance or teachings to others about good living. Warok is a person who has perfected their actions and reached inner peace (warok is a person who has perfected their actions in life and reached inner peace). Warok is a character or trait and spirit of the Ponorogo community that has been ingrained since ancient times and passed down from ancestors to future generations. Warok is a part of the Reog art performance that cannot be separated from other performers in the Reog Ponorogo art unit.
